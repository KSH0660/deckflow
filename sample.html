Risks, Sensitivities & Mitigations

Key risks are quantifiable and mitigatable via staged investments, localization, diversified monetization, and KPI gating.

Audience: C-Suite · Labels · Investors

Risks — principal

  • Content–market fit & UA: CAC spikes, weak engagement
  • Monetization timing: slow conversion/retention
  • Regulatory & localization: payments, compliance friction
Sensitivity examples
+30% CAC → ~20% fewer Year‑1 paying users (fixed budget)
25–40% lower conversion → Year‑1 revenue −$0.4–0.9M
No local payments in SEA → conversion −up to 35%

Mitigations & gating

  • Staged rollouts + controlled spend
  • Partnership-first: regional publishers, DSPs, promoters
  • Diversified revenue: ads, merch, live, licensing
